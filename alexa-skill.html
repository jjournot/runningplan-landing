Running Plan

Running Plan Alexa Skill wants to help you to reach your running goal

How does it work?

Configuration

Enter your running goal. All the information the skill needs is the distance you are planning to run and the date when you plan to achieve it.

Train

Add the skill to your morning routine or open it daily to get the running instructions. Record your training with any smart device and sync it with Strava for personnalized feedback.

Succeed

Follow the plan until your race day and enjoy seeing your progress. 3 days prior to the race, you will also get few nutrition tips to perform at your best.

If you feel that you need a little extra time, you can purchase the warm up program which will help you to ramp up your physical condition.

Create an account

You can enhance your experience by linking your Strava account to the skill. The skill will then be able to check if you are trully following the plan and either encourage or scold you.
